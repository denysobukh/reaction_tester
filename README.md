# Reaction Tester

A simple web app to measure your reaction time.

## How to Play

1. A yellow square appears — wait patiently.
2. When it turns **green**, tap or click as fast as you can.
3. Your reaction time in milliseconds is displayed.
4. If you tap too early, you get a **red penalty** and must wait 3 seconds before the next round.
5. Each round restarts automatically.
